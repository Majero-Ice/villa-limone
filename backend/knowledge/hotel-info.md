# Villa Limone - Hotel Information

## About Us

Villa Limone is a charming boutique hotel nestled on the stunning Ligurian coast of Italy. Our family-owned property combines traditional Italian hospitality with modern comfort, offering guests an unforgettable Mediterranean experience.

## Location

We are ideally located for exploring the best of the Italian Riviera:
- Cinque Terre: 20 minutes by car
- Portofino: 15 minutes by car
- Genoa: 45 minutes by car
- Private beach access: 2-minute walk from the hotel

The hotel is situated in a quiet residential area with beautiful sea and garden views, offering the perfect balance of tranquility and accessibility to nearby attractions.

## Breakfast

Our complimentary breakfast is served daily from 7:30 AM to 10:30 AM in our dining room or on the terrace (weather permitting). We offer a selection of:
- Fresh homemade pastries and bread
- Local cheeses and cold cuts
- Fresh seasonal fruits
- Italian coffee, tea, and fresh juices
- Homemade jams and honey from local producers
