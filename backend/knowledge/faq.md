# Villa Limone - Frequently Asked Questions

## Booking & Reservations

### How do I make a reservation?
You can book directly through our website, by email at info@villalimone.it, or by phone at +39 0185 123 456. We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance during peak season (June-September).

### What is the minimum stay requirement?
During high season (June-August), we require a minimum stay of 3 nights. During shoulder and low season, there is no minimum stay requirement.

### Can I modify my reservation after booking?
Yes, modifications are free of charge if made more than 7 days before arrival. Changes within 7 days of arrival are subject to availability and may incur fees.

### Do you offer gift vouchers?
Yes, we offer gift vouchers for stays or specific experiences (cooking classes, boat tours, spa treatments). Contact us directly to purchase.

## Rooms & Amenities

### Which room has the best view?
Camera Mare offers the most stunning sea views with a private balcony overlooking the Ligurian coast. Suite Portofino also has panoramic sea and coastal views plus a private jacuzzi on the terrace.

### Can I request a specific room?
Yes, you can request a specific room when booking. We will do our best to accommodate your preference, but it cannot be guaranteed.

### Is there a bathtub in any room?
Suite Portofino has a jacuzzi tub in one of its two bathrooms. All other rooms have showers only.

### Do rooms have a coffee maker?
Rooms do not have coffee makers, but complimentary breakfast includes excellent Italian coffee. Room service also offers coffee from 7:00 AM.

### Are the rooms soundproof?
Our rooms have good sound insulation. Camera Giardino is our quietest room, located in the most peaceful area of the property overlooking the courtyard.

### Can I get an extra bed or crib?
Extra beds for children under 12 are €30/night. Baby cribs are provided free of charge upon request. Please inform us when booking.

## Check-in & Check-out

### Can I check in early or check out late?
Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability. There may be an additional charge of €30-50 depending on the time requested.

### What if my flight arrives late at night?
Reception is available 24/7. Please inform us of your expected arrival time so we can ensure someone is ready to welcome you.

### Can I store luggage before check-in or after check-out?
Yes, complimentary luggage storage is available on your arrival and departure days.

## Dining & Food

### Is breakfast included?
Yes, a full Italian breakfast is included with all room rates, served daily from 7:30 AM to 10:30 AM.

### Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?
Absolutely. Please inform us of any allergies or dietary requirements (vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, etc.) at least 24 hours in advance.

### Is the restaurant open every day?
Our restaurant is open for dinner Tuesday through Sunday, 6:00 PM to 10:00 PM. It is closed on Mondays. Room service is available daily.

### Can I book the restaurant for a special occasion?
Yes, we can arrange special dinners, birthday celebrations, or anniversary meals. Contact our concierge to discuss your requirements.

### Do you have a wine list?
Yes, our wine cellar features an excellent selection of Ligurian wines, including local Vermentino and Pigato, as well as wines from other Italian regions.

## Beach & Pool

### Do you have a swimming pool?
We do not have a pool, but our private beach area is just a 2-minute walk from the hotel with calm, clear waters perfect for swimming.

### Is the beach sandy or rocky?
Our beach area has a mix of pebbles and sand. We recommend water shoes for comfort, which are available at reception.

### Are beach towels provided?
Yes, beach towels are provided free of charge and exchanged daily.

### Can I reserve a sun lounger?
Sun loungers at our beach area are available on a first-come, first-served basis for hotel guests. They are rarely all occupied.

### Is the beach suitable for children?
Yes, the water is calm and shallow near the shore, making it suitable for children. However, adult supervision is always required.

## Transportation & Parking

### Is parking available?
Yes, free on-site parking is available for all guests. No reservation required.

### How do I get to the hotel from Genoa Airport?
The easiest option is our airport transfer service (€80 one way). Alternatively, you can take a train from Genoa to our nearest station and then a short taxi ride.

### Do I need a car to explore the area?
A car is convenient but not essential. Many attractions are accessible by train, bus, or boat. We can arrange car rental if needed.

### Can you arrange a taxi?
Yes, reception can arrange taxis 24/7. We recommend booking in advance for early morning departures.

## Activities & Excursions

### What activities can you arrange?
Our concierge can arrange: boat tours, wine tasting, cooking classes, hiking excursions, e-bike rentals, kayaking, spa treatments, yoga sessions, and restaurant reservations.

### How far in advance should I book activities?
We recommend booking popular activities (cooking classes, boat tours) at least 3-5 days in advance, especially during high season.

### Is Cinque Terre worth visiting?
Absolutely! Cinque Terre is one of Italy's most beautiful destinations, just 20 minutes from our hotel. We recommend going early morning or late afternoon to avoid crowds.

### What is the best way to visit Portofino?
You can drive (15 minutes) or take a scenic boat trip. Parking in Portofino is expensive and limited, so we recommend the boat or our shuttle service.

### Can I rent a boat without a license?
Yes, small boats (up to 40 HP) can be rented without a license in Italy. We can arrange rentals starting from €80 for 3 hours.

## Policies

### Is smoking allowed?
Villa Limone is a non-smoking property indoors. Smoking is permitted on terraces, balconies, and in garden areas.

### Are pets allowed?
Small pets are welcome for an additional fee of €20 per night. Please inform us when booking. We provide pet beds and bowls upon request.

### What are the quiet hours?
We ask guests to keep noise to a minimum between 10:00 PM and 8:00 AM.

### Is the hotel accessible for wheelchair users?
We have ground floor rooms available and wheelchair-accessible common areas. An elevator provides access to all floors. Please contact us to discuss specific requirements.

## Payment & Pricing

### When do I pay for my stay?
A credit card is required to guarantee the reservation. Full payment is due upon check-out.

### What currency do you accept?
We accept Euros. Credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) are also accepted.

### Are prices per room or per person?
All room prices are per room per night, not per person. Breakfast is included for all registered guests.

### Do children stay free?
Children under 2 stay free when using existing bedding. Children 2-12 years are charged €30/night for an extra bed.

## Weather & Best Time to Visit

### What is the best time to visit?
May-June and September-October offer the best combination of pleasant weather, fewer crowds, and full access to all activities. July-August are warmest but busiest.

### Is it warm enough to swim in May?
Sea temperatures in May average 18-20°C. Most guests find it comfortable for swimming, though some prefer to wait until June when it reaches 22-24°C.

### What should I pack?
Light, breathable clothing for summer. Comfortable walking shoes for exploring villages and hiking. A light jacket for cooler evenings, even in summer.

## Special Requests

### Can you arrange a marriage proposal?
Yes! We love helping create special moments. Our concierge can arrange private dinners, sunset boat trips, flower arrangements, and more. Contact us to plan your perfect proposal.

### Do you host weddings?
We do not host large weddings, but we can arrange intimate ceremonies for small groups (up to 20 guests). Contact us for details.

### Can you arrange a surprise for my partner's birthday?
Absolutely. We can organize cakes, flowers, champagne, room decorations, special dinners, or excursions. Please let us know at least 48 hours in advance.
