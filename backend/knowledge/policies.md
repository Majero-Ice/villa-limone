# Villa Limone - Policies & Information

## Cancellation Policy

- Free cancellation up to 7 days before arrival
- Cancellations made 3-7 days before arrival: 50% of total booking charged
- Cancellations made less than 3 days before arrival: 100% of total booking charged
- No-shows: 100% of total booking charged

## Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:
- Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
- Debit cards
- Bank transfer (must be received 7 days before arrival)
- Cash (Euros only)

A credit card is required at booking to guarantee the reservation. Payment is due upon check-out.

## Children Policy

- Children of all ages are welcome
- Children under 2 years stay free of charge when using existing bedding
- Children 2-12 years: €30 per night for extra bed
- Children 12+ are charged adult rates

We provide:
- Baby cribs (free, upon request)
- High chairs
- Children's menu at restaurant
- Babysitting services (upon request, additional fee)

## Smoking Policy

Villa Limone is a non-smoking property. Smoking is not permitted inside any rooms or indoor common areas. Designated smoking areas are available:
- Terrace
- Garden areas
- Balconies

## Quiet Hours

To ensure all guests enjoy a peaceful stay, we request that noise be kept to a minimum between 10:00 PM and 8:00 AM.

## Restaurant & Dining

Our on-site restaurant is open:
- Dinner: 6:00 PM - 10:00 PM (Tuesday - Sunday)
- Closed on Mondays
- Reservations recommended

Room service is available from 7:00 AM - 10:00 PM daily. Menu available in room.

## Special Services

### Airport Transfer
We offer airport transfer services to/from:
- Genoa Airport (Cristoforo Colombo): €80 one way
- Nice Airport: €150 one way
- Milan Malpensa Airport: €200 one way

### Laundry Service
Available Monday-Friday. Same-day service available for items dropped off before 10:00 AM (additional fee applies).

### Massage Services
In-room massage services available upon request (24-hour advance booking recommended).

## Beach Access

Villa Limone provides access to our private beach area (2-minute walk). We provide:
- Beach towels (one per guest, exchanged daily)
- Sun loungers
- Beach umbrellas

Beach facilities are available from 8:00 AM to 7:00 PM during season (May - September).

## Weather

The Ligurian coast enjoys a Mediterranean climate:
- Spring (March-May): 15-22°C, mild and pleasant
- Summer (June-August): 25-30°C, warm and sunny
- Fall (September-November): 18-24°C, warm and comfortable
- Winter (December-February): 8-15°C, mild winters

Best time to visit: May-June and September-October for pleasant weather and fewer crowds.
