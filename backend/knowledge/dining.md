# Villa Limone - Dining Guide

## Our Restaurant

Villa Limone's restaurant showcases the best of Ligurian cuisine, prepared with locally-sourced ingredients and traditional recipes passed down through the Rossi family.

### Hours
- Dinner: 6:00 PM - 10:00 PM (Tuesday - Sunday)
- Closed Mondays
- Reservations recommended for hotel guests and required for outside visitors

### The Menu

Our menu changes seasonally to reflect the freshest available ingredients. Signature dishes include:

**Antipasti**
- Cappon magro: Traditional Ligurian seafood and vegetable salad
- Burrata with San Marzano tomatoes and basil oil
- Local anchovies marinated in lemon and olive oil
- Focaccia di Recco with stracchino cheese

**Primi**
- Trofie al pesto Genovese: Fresh pasta with authentic basil pesto (our most requested dish)
- Pansoti with walnut sauce: Ravioli filled with local herbs and ricotta
- Risotto ai frutti di mare: Seafood risotto with daily catch
- Minestrone alla Genovese: Traditional vegetable soup with pesto

**Secondi**
- Branzino al forno: Oven-roasted sea bass with Ligurian olives
- Fritto misto: Mixed fried seafood with seasonal vegetables
- Coniglio alla Ligure: Rabbit braised with pine nuts and Taggiasca olives
- Grilled catch of the day with herb sauce

**Dolci**
- Pandolce Genovese: Traditional Genoese sweet bread
- Lemon panna cotta with our grove's limoncello
- Tiramisu made with local mascarpone
- Selection of local cheeses with honey

### Wine List

Our wine cellar features over 50 labels, with emphasis on Ligurian wines:

**White Wines**
- Vermentino: Crisp, mineral, perfect with seafood
- Pigato: Fuller-bodied, aromatic, pairs well with pesto dishes
- Cinque Terre DOC: Rare local white from the famous terraces

**Red Wines**
- Rossese di Dolceacqua: Light, elegant Ligurian red
- Selection of wines from Piedmont, Tuscany, and other Italian regions

**Dessert Wines**
- Sciacchetrà: Rare sweet wine from Cinque Terre (€15/glass)

Corkage fee for outside bottles: €20
